Self Reviser presents the same personal experience in two parallel forms: a draft that remains closer to lived experience and immediate reflection on the left, and an institutionally interpreted narrative on the right.

The work invites viewers to read between these two forms of expression and consider: as a narrative becomes increasingly precise and coherent, does it also begin to change the way we understand ourselves? Which version feels closer to who we are?
